About us

Honeysuckle Harvest is a vegan restaurant that prioritizes the health, product quality, and sustainability of our offerings. Our ingredients are locally sourced.

Our name developed because of the abundance of the Honeysuckle flower in Georgia. Our ingredients are also heavily influenced by Georgiaâ€™s crops and agriculture. We wanted to make sure the quality of our ingredients was the best it could be, therefore we picked ingredients that could be sourced locally.

Every day the number of vegans and vegetarians increase, so we created Honeysuckle Harvest to not only increase our local vegan options but also to support our local economy by using ingredients grown in Georgia.

By partnering with local producers our restaurant can guarantee that we are catering to you the freshest meals, it reduces our carbon footprint, we ensure every ingredient is cruelty free and aligns with vegan values.

By choosing Honeysuckle Harvest you are making a positive impact in our local community.
